### How do I see my active and past loans?

To see your active loans:
1. Sign in to your account.
2. Choose a loan in the *Active loans* section.

To see your past loans:
1. Sign in to your account.
2. Click or tap on *View past loans* in the *Active Loans* section.
3. Choose a loan.
